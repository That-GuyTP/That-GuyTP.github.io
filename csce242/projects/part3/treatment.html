Treatment

Sadly Narcolepsy does not currently have a cure. While it can be treated, it is a life long condition and will take a lot of patience and time to learn how to manage life with. The best recommendation is to visit a trusted Sleep Doctor to assist with providing medical solutions. Many narcolepstics

Lifestyle Changes

While Narcolespy may be uncurable there are many things you can do without the need of medicine to help manage your conditions:

  1. Mantain a regular sleep schedule
  2. Eat healthy and stay in shape
  3. Take naps
  4. Reach out to others
